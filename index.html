About Us

Ignite Show Systems is a scenic design company dedicated to bringing creative visions to life through expertly crafted stage and set solutions. With years of experience in theatrical productions, live events, and immersive experiences, our team combines technical skill, innovative design, and a passion for storytelling.

Currently, we are designing and building the set for Matilda the Musical, creating a visually striking and functional environment that brings Roald Dahl's world to life on stage. From the quirky Wormwood house to the lively school scenes, every piece is designed to enhance the performance and immerse the audience in the story.

Our commitment to quality, reliability, and creativity makes us a trusted partner for theaters, schools, event companies, and independent productions seeking professional scenic solutions. At Ignite Show Systems, we don’t just build sets—we create experiences that leave a lasting impression.
